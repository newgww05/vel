One Roll, Every Length — Zero Waste.

Every pre-cut island dressing comes with the same hidden cost: dimensional mismatch. A 3 cm finger laceration forces the use of a 5×7.5 cm island, half of which is wasted margin. A 20 cm post-operative incision forces two overlapping pieces and a visible seam. Velcura Non-woven Adhesive Wound Dressing Strip eliminates both — a continuous 10-metre roll with a built-in absorbent pad layer running the full length, cut at the bedside to exactly the length each wound needs. One roll, one product code, one SKU on the shelf — every wound dressed to perfect size.

The construction matches the familiar comfort of our pre-cut non-woven dressing: soft porous spunlace non-woven backing, hypoallergenic latex-free acrylic adhesive, non-adherent absorbent pad. The format is what changes. Four widths — 2.5, 5, 7.5, 10 cm — cover everything from a finger strip to broader incision coverage, and the 10-metre length means a single roll handles many wounds over many days. For high-volume clinics, home wound-care kits, and field-medic packs, it is the most economical way to keep a flexible dressing supply on hand.

Continuous Cut-to-Length Roll

A single 10-metre roll, cut at the bedside to exactly the length each wound requires. Eliminates the dimensional waste of pre-cut island dressings — 3 cm wound takes 3 cm of strip, 20 cm wound takes 20 cm. No half-empty packets, no surplus margin, no over-cut.

Built-in Absorbent Pad Layer

An absorbent pad layer runs the entire length of the roll — the wound bed contacts non-adherent material no matter where the strip is cut. No need to add separate gauze or change product mid-application; the roll behaves like a single continuous island dressing.

Soft Non-woven, Familiar Comfort

The same soft porous spunlace non-woven backing as our pre-cut Non-woven Adhesive Dressing — breathable, skin-friendly, conformable. Hypoallergenic latex-free acrylic adhesive holds without irritating periwound skin and lifts cleanly without residue.

One Roll, Many Wounds

10 metres per roll, four widths — a single SKU handles dozens of dressing changes across multiple wounds and multiple patients. The economical choice for high-volume clinics, GP surgeries, home wound kits, ambulance packs, and field-medic supplies.

Cautions
  • Cut with sharp clean scissors against a clean surface — a frayed or contaminated cut edge compromises both the seal and the sterility of the wound contact area.
  • Low-absorbency pad layer only — not suitable for moderately or heavily exuding wounds. Choose a foam or alginate dressing for higher exudate.
  • Non-sterile roll — apply only as a secondary cover or over a sterile primary dressing. Do not place the cut pad area directly on a deep or surgical open wound without an intermediate sterile contact layer.
  • Water-resistant but not waterproof — replace after bathing, swimming, or saturation by perspiration or exudate.
  • Do not stretch the strip during application — tension trapped under the adhesive creates blistering, edge lift, and skin shear.
  • Remove slowly, parallel to the skin (not upward), supporting the underlying tissue — the standard MARSI-prevention removal technique.
  • Store the roll in its plastic shell between uses; protect the exposed end from dust and contamination.

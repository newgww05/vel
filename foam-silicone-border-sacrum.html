Shaped to the Sacrum — Sealing the Natal Cleft and Reducing the Shear Behind Pressure Injuries

Velcura Foam Dressing Silicone Adhesive Border (Sacrum) is a five-layer soft-silicone bordered foam dressing anatomically contoured for the sacrum and coccyx. Its shape follows the natal cleft for full, secure coverage, while the soft silicone border seals the edges against the skin — keeping exudate in and preventing it from leaking onto vulnerable peri-wound skin. The absorbent foam core manages exudate and a waterproof, breathable film backing provides a bacterial barrier.

Where a standard rectangular border dressing struggles to stay sealed over a curved, high-movement area, the sacrum shape is engineered to fit the contour and to reduce the friction and shear forces that drive pressure-injury formation — so it suits both prophylactic use on at-risk skin and the management of established sacral pressure injuries. The silicone border lifts and repositions for skin checks and removes atraumatically.

Anatomical Sacrum Shape

Contoured to the sacrum and coccyx, the dressing follows the natal cleft for full coverage and a secure seal where rectangular dressings tend to lift and roll.

Reduces Shear & Friction

The shaped, low-profile design and gliding backing reduce the friction and shear forces at the sacrum that are key drivers of pressure-injury formation, supporting prophylactic prevention in at-risk patients.

Soft Silicone Border Seal

A gentle silicone border seals the wound edges to keep exudate off the surrounding skin, secures without separate fixation, and lifts and repositions for skin checks with atraumatic, pain-free removal.

Absorbent Foam, Waterproof Backing

The multi-layer foam core absorbs and locks exudate to reduce maceration, while the waterproof, breathable film backing keeps out fluids and microbes and allows showering.

Cautions
  • Apply to clean, dry, intact peri-wound skin; not a treatment for clinically infected wounds unless directed by a clinician.
  • Inspect the skin regularly; lift and reposition the silicone border gently rather than peeling it abruptly.
  • Not for third-degree burns or wounds with active heavy bleeding.
  • Discontinue immediately and seek medical advice if any abnormal reaction occurs.

Velcura® Nano-Silver Medical Antibacterial Dressing

Nano-Silver Medical Antibacterial Dressing provides strong antimicrobial protection to help reduce infection risk and support faster healing. Infused with nano-silver particles, it delivers long-lasting antibacterial action while maintaining a moist wound healing environment. Suitable for chronic wounds, surgical incisions, and burns, this dressing promotes optimal recovery and patient comfort.

  • Nano-silver for antimicrobial protection
  • Reduces infection risk, supports healing
  • Maintains moist wound environment
  • Suitable for chronic, surgical, and burn wounds

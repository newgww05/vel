Breathable Where Film Seals, Strong Where Paper Tears — The Everyday Workhorse of Medical Tape.

Across every tape drawer in every clinic, the same picture repeats: paper tape for sensitive skin, transparent film when waterproofing matters, zinc oxide or cotton when rigid support is needed — and a roll of soft non-woven tape that handles everything in between. Velcura Non-woven Surgical Tape is built for that everyday majority: the 80% of dressing-fixation jobs where you need something breathable enough to leave on for days, strong enough to hold a gauze pad through a shift, gentle enough not to wreck the skin underneath, and quick enough to tear by hand at the bedside.

The microporous spunlace polyester backing breathes like paper but holds like cloth. The hypoallergenic acrylic adhesive — latex-free, zinc-oxide-free — fixes securely yet lifts cleanly without residue or skin tearing. The non-woven structure has just enough natural give to follow contours of joints, abdomens, and paediatric limbs without bunching. It is the tape that earns its place in every kit because it does almost every job well enough — without forcing the clinician to choose a specialty product.

Microporous Spunlace Backing

Soft non-woven polyester (or polyester-rayon blend) creates natural micro-channels for air and moisture vapour to escape — a defining advantage over PE film and zinc oxide tapes, which seal the skin and risk maceration. Breathability you don't have to engineer into the adhesive.

Hypoallergenic, Latex-Free Adhesive

A pressure-sensitive acrylic adhesive — free of latex, free of zinc oxide — minimises the contact-dermatitis and irritation reactions historically associated with rubber and zinc-oxide tape systems. Suitable for sensitive skin, paediatrics, and patients with known latex sensitivity.

Conformable, Yet Hand-Tearable

The non-woven structure has natural give — it follows the contours of knees, elbows, abdomens, and paediatric limbs without bunching or buckling. And unlike cotton or zinc-oxide cloth, it tears cleanly by hand across the grain — no scissors, no jagged edges, no delay at the bedside.

Strong Hold, Clean Removal

Adhesion is calibrated to hold gauze pads, foam dressings, IV gauze, and lightweight tubing securely through normal daily activity — yet lifts without residue, fibre shedding, or skin-stripping. Stronger than paper tape; gentler at change than silk or zinc oxide.

Cautions
  • Apply only to clean, dry, intact skin — do not place adhesive directly over broken, infected, or actively bleeding wounds.
  • Do not stretch the tape during application — tension trapped under the adhesive creates blistering, edge lift, and skin shear injuries.
  • Remove slowly, parallel to the skin surface (not upward) — to reduce Medical Adhesive-Related Skin Injury (MARSI), particularly in elderly or steroid-affected skin.
  • Replace the tape if adhesion is lost through prolonged exposure to perspiration, water, or exudate — non-woven tape is water-resistant, not waterproof.
  • For known acrylic-adhesive sensitivity, test on a small area first; discontinue and switch to a silicone-adhesive tape if local reaction occurs.
  • Non-sterile — do not use as a primary dressing in contact with open wound beds; pair with a sterile primary dressing.

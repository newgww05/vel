A Keyhole Cut That Cradles the Tube — Wicking Secretions Away From Peristomal Skin

Foam Dressing Non-Adhesive features a fully adhesive-free design: a soft, non-adherent polyurethane foam contacts the wound directly with no silicone, acrylic or hydrocolloid adhesive of any kind. The hydrophilic foam core delivers high exudate absorption, while a waterproof breathable outer film maintains the moist healing environment. Designed to be secured with bandages, tape, netting or compression systems — ideal where adhesive contact must be avoided.

Especially indicated for venous leg ulcers and lymphedema under compression bandaging, for patients with silicone or adhesive sensitivities, for very fragile or thin skin where any adhesive contact risks tearing, and for irregularly shaped wounds where the dressing must be trimmed to fit. Suitable as a secondary absorbent layer over contact-layer dressings.

Keyhole / Fenestrated Opening

A pre-cut keyhole fits neatly around tracheostomy tubes, drains and other indwelling devices, sitting flush against the skin without kinking the tube.

Absorbs Secretions, Protects Skin

The absorbent foam wicks secretions and exudate away from the stoma, helping prevent peristomal maceration and keeping the surrounding skin dry.

Cushions the Device, Sheds No Fibres

Soft foam cushions the tube against the skin and reduces pressure, while the foam structure sheds no loose fibres around the airway; non-adhesive and secured with ties or tape.

Waterproof, Breathable Backing

A waterproof, breathable film backing resists strike-through and external contamination while letting the skin breathe.

Cautions
  • Non-adhesive — must be secured with tracheostomy ties or tape.
  • Not a treatment for clinically infected stoma sites unless directed by a clinician.
  • Monitor the airway and secretions; change when saturated to prevent peristomal maceration.
  • Discontinue immediately and seek medical advice if any abnormal reaction occurs.

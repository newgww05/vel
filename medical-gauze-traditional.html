Core Features

The Dependable Choice in
Foundation Dressings

Velcura Medical Gauze Swab is built on traditional absorbent-cotton craft. Combing, bleaching, weaving and scouring remove natural cottonseed oil, wax and impurities while preserving the fibre's inherent absorbency and softness.

As the most widely used traditional dressing in clinical practice, gauze swabs are indispensable for debridement, wound coverage, fluid absorption, bandaging and intra-operative support. Sterile-packed and single-use, they are a staple in hospitals, clinics and home first-aid kits.

High-Density Absorbent Cotton

Woven from 100% pure cotton and scoured and bleached to strip out cottonseed oil and impurities. The fibre is clean and soft with a near-neutral pH, non-irritating to skin and mucosa.

High Absorbency — Rapid Exudate Lock

The multi-layer folded structure delivers excellent fluid capacity, rapidly taking up wound exudate and blood to keep the wound surface clean and reduce the risk of maceration and contamination.

Breathable — No Heat Build-Up

The open mesh fibre structure lets air and water vapour pass through, preventing heat and moisture build-up at the wound, maintaining a balanced microenvironment and working well as a secondary dressing.

Sterile and Reliable — Single Use

Individually sealed in sterile sachets and ethylene-oxide sterilised. Every swab passes strict QC for neat edges, no loose threads and no foreign matter — ready for single clinical use.

Cautions
  • Sterile single-use packaging — do not use if the pack is damaged or past its expiry date.
  • Not recommended for prolonged contact with heavily exuding wounds, as fibres may adhere to tissue. Where moist wound healing is required, choose an advanced dressing such as a hydrocolloid or alginate.
  • When packing deep wounds or cavities, count every swab and confirm complete removal to avoid retention.
  • Use with caution in patients allergic to cotton fibre; discontinue immediately and seek medical advice if any adverse reaction occurs.

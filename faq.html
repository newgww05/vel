Frequently Asked Questions

How we
partner with you

Common questions from distributors, hospital procurement and private-label brand partners working with Velcura Medical.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for Velcura wound dressings? +
Our standard MOQ varies by product line and packaging configuration. For standard stock items, MOQ typically starts from 5,000 pieces per SKU. For OEM/ODM and private-label orders with custom packaging, MOQ generally starts from 10,000 pieces per SKU. Final MOQ is confirmed during quotation based on specifications. We also support small trial orders for first-time partners — contact our sales team to discuss your requirements.
Do you offer OEM and ODM services for wound care products? +
Yes. Velcura Medical is a full-service OEM and ODM partner. OEM service includes manufacturing under your brand with our existing product specifications, custom packaging, custom labelling and your brand identity. ODM service includes co-development of new formulations, sizes or product configurations tailored to your market — from concept and prototyping through clinical-grade production. We work with hospital procurement groups, distributors and private-label brands worldwide.
What is your sample policy? +
Free product samples are provided for qualified business buyers — typically up to 3-5 pieces per requested SKU. Shipping costs are paid by the buyer (we accept your courier account or prepayment). Samples are dispatched within 3-5 working days of confirmation. For custom OEM samples (your branding, custom packaging), tooling fees and minimum sample quantities apply. Contact info@velcura.net with your business profile and product list.
Can packaging and labelling be customised? +
Yes — Velcura offers full custom packaging and labelling services. Options include custom box and pouch design, multi-language labelling (English, Spanish, French, Arabic, Russian and others), barcode and lot-number printing, custom carton specifications and shipping marks. Regulatory text and symbols are formatted to match your destination market requirements. Standard turnaround for custom packaging design proofs is 7-10 working days from artwork confirmation.
What is the typical production lead time? +
Standard production lead time is 25-35 working days from order confirmation and 30% deposit. For stock items, dispatch is within 7-10 working days. For OEM orders with custom packaging, allow an additional 7-10 days for packaging artwork approval and production. Large-volume orders (above 100,000 units per SKU) may require 40-50 working days. Sea-freight transit time is not included in the production lead time.
What shipping methods and Incoterms do you support? +
We ship globally from Shenzhen, China. Supported Incoterms include EXW, FOB, CIF and DDP — selected based on order size and destination. For small samples and urgent orders, we use international express (DHL, FedEx, UPS) with 3-5 day delivery. For bulk orders we use sea freight (LCL or full container) with typical transit times of 20-35 days depending on destination port. All shipments include commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin and product certification documents as required by destination customs.

Have another question?

Our team typically responds within one business day.
